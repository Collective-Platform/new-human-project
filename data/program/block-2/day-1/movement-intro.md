## MOVEMENT 1 — New Joy

# Joy that holds even when circumstances do not

Days 1–7 · Philippians 1:1–2:18

---

Ephesians showed us who we are in Christ and what he has built. Philippians shows us how the new humanity actually feels from the inside, not triumphant and comfortable, but deeply joyful even under pressure. These seven days trace Paul's extraordinary joy from a Roman prison cell, and ask what it looks like for a congregation to share it.
