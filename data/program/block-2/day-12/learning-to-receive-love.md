## Learning to Receive Love

## Scripture:

_We love because he first loved us._ \- 1 John 4:19

Many people know God loves them.

Fewer people actually live from that love.

We often feel we need to earn affection through performance.

Yet God loved us before we achieved anything.

The more secure we become in God's love, the healthier our emotional world becomes.

## Practice:

Spend five minutes meditating on God's love.

## Question:

Do I believe God loves me because of who He is or because of what I do?
