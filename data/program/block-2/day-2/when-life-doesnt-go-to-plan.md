## Today's Focus

How does the new humanity respond when everything works against it?

## Reading Notes

Paul is in prison. His ministry has been disrupted by forces outside his control. And yet he says something surprising. He tells us that what has happened to him has actually helped spread the gospel. The whole imperial guard knows about Jesus, and other believers have become much braver. Even when people preach out of rivalry and envy, Paul refuses to be bitter about it, because Christ is being preached. This is not naive optimism or denial. It is a deep conviction that God is working even through what feels like a massive setback. The new humanity is not a community that pretends everything is fine. It is a community that has learned to look for God's movement even in hard times.

## Key Idea

_The new humanity does not need perfect circumstances in order to rejoice. God is at work even in what looks like a setback._

## Reflection

Is there a situation in your life or church right now that feels like a setback? What might God be doing through it that you haven't seen yet?

## Today's Practice

Write down one difficulty you are currently facing. Then write one way God might be at work even in that situation. Pray over it.
