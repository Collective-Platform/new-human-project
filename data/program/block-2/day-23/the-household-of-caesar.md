## Today's Focus

The new humanity grows in the most unexpected places.

## Reading Notes

Paul's closing greetings include a surprising detail. He mentions that people from Caesar’s household send their love. In the very center of imperial power, inside the administration that put Paul in prison, there are members of the new humanity. The gospel has reached into Caesar's own court. The new humanity is not confined to religious spaces or cozy communities. It is quietly growing in places no one expected. This is a huge encouragement if you work in a secular or hostile environment. You are not alone, and your presence there is not an accident. God is building his community in the most unlikely soil.

## Key Idea

_The new humanity grows in unexpected places, even the household of those who oppose it. Your workplace, your neighbourhood, your family is not beyond its reach._

## Reflection

Where in your life do you feel most like the only Christian in the room? How does knowing that the gospel reached Caesar's household encourage you?

## Today's Practice

Pray today for two or three specific people in your workplace or neighbourhood who don't yet know Christ. Ask God to be at work in their lives in ways you can't see yet.
