## MOVEMENT 2 — New Humility

# The people who hold the new humanity together

Days 8–13 · Philippians 2:19–3:14

---

The new humanity is not held together by structures or programs — it is held together by people. People who are genuinely other-centred, who count others more significant than themselves, who have given up their credentials before Christ. These six days move from portraits of faithful people to Paul's most personal confession: that everything he once was proud of, he counts as loss for the surpassing worth of knowing Jesus.
