## Today's Focus

Anxiety is addressed not by trying harder, but by bringing everything to God in prayer.

## Reading Notes

Do not be anxious about anything but in everything by prayer and supplication with thanksgiving let your requests be made known to God. And the promise: the peace of God, which surpasses all understanding, will guard your hearts and minds in Christ Jesus. Paul is not offering a technique for managing stress. He is describing a relationship. Anxiety is not conquered by willpower or positive thinking. It is transformed by coming to God with honesty and gratitude — and receiving something back that you cannot manufacture. This peace is not the absence of difficulty. It is a guard, standing watch over the heart and mind even in the middle of the storm. The new humanity lives from this place.

## Key Idea

_The peace the new humanity lives from is not the absence of problems. It is the presence of God guarding the heart in the middle of them._

## Reflection

What are the anxieties you tend to carry alone rather than bring to God? What would it look like to pray about them with thanksgiving rather than with fear?

## Today's Practice

Write down your three biggest current anxieties. Bring each one to God explicitly in prayer today with thanksgiving for his care. Leave them with Him.
