## Today's Focus

What does it actually mean to know Christ, not just know _about_ him?

## Reading Notes

Paul uses the most extreme language possible. He says his old achievements are nothing but garbage, treating them like literal waste compared to gaining Christ. He doesn't want his own religious record anymore. He only wants the right standing with God that comes through faith. This is the gospel at its core. Paul isn't chasing success; he is chasing a person. To be found in Jesus is the only thing that matters. The new humanity is a community of people who have staked everything on this single truth: knowing Christ is better than anything else the world has to offer.

## Key Idea

_The goal of the Christian life is not a better version of yourself. It is knowing Christ and being found in him._

## Reflection

If someone observed your daily life, your time, your ambitions, your worries would they conclude that knowing Christ is your surpassing aim?

## Today's Practice

Pray Philippians 3:8-9 as your own today. Ask God to deepen your actual experience of knowing Christ, not just knowing about him.
