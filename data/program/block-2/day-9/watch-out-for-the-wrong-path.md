## Today's Focus

Not everything that looks spiritual is. The new humanity needs to know the difference.

## Reading Notes

Paul's tone shifts sharply. He warns the Philippians, with real urgency about those who would lead them away from grace and back to a religion of credentials, performance, and outward markers. He calls them, bluntly, dogs and evildoers. These are people who insist that belonging to God requires jumping through the right religious hoops. But Paul says the true circumcision is the true marker of God's people. It is those who worship by the Spirit, glory in Christ Jesus, and put no confidence in the flesh. The new humanity does not earn its place. It receives it. Any teaching that adds conditions to grace is a threat to the community Christ has built.

## Key Idea

_The new humanity stands on grace alone. Any path that says 'Christ plus your credentials' is a path away from Jesus._

## Reflection

Does your community have an invisible checklist for belonging? How can you replace those standards with grace alone?

## Today's Practice

Ask yourself honestly: is there anything you are secretly relying on for your standing before God other than Christ? Name it, and surrender it in prayer.
