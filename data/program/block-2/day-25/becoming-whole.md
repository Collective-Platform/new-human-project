## Becoming Whole

## Scripture:

_So then, just as you received Christ Jesus as Lord, continue to live your lives in him, rooted and built up in him, strengthened in the faith as you were taught, and overflowing with thankfulness._ \- Colossians 2:6-7

The goal was never emotional perfection. The goal was always wholeness.

A life where your emotions, relationships, thoughts, body, and spirituality are increasingly surrendered to Jesus.

You will still have difficult days.

You will still have moments where you select "ANGRY" or "SAD" on your mood tracker.

But now you know those emotions are not signs of failure.

They are invitations. Invitations to bring your whole self to God.

The New Humanity is not about becoming flawless. It is about becoming whole.

## Practice:

Review the last 25 days and write down your biggest takeaway.

## Question:

1. How has God been forming me through this journey?
2. What rhythms do I want to continue beyond these 25 days?
3. What kind of person am I becoming?
