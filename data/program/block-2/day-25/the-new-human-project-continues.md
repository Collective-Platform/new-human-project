## Today's Focus

What has been started in you will be completed. The project is not finished yet.

## Reading Notes

Go back to Philippians 1:6: I am confident that he who began a good work in you will bring it to completion at the day of Jesus Christ. This is the great promise that holds the entire New Human Project together. God started something in Ephesus, in Philippi, in your church — and he will finish it. Not because you are faithful enough, not because your community is impressive enough, but because he is faithful. You have now walked fifty days through two of Paul's most beautiful letters. You have seen the new humanity described from the outside — what God has built — and from the inside — how it feels to live it. Now go. Be the new humanity. The project continues through you.

## Key Idea

_He who began a good work in you will bring it to completion. The New Human Project is not yours to finish but you are part of it, and it will not fail._

## Reflection

After 50 days in Ephesians and Philippians combined, what is the single most important thing you want to carry forward into the rest of your life as a member of the new humanity?

## Today's Practice

Gather with at least one other person from your church this week. Share what God has done in you through this study. Pray for each other. And ask: what is the next step for us together as the new humanity?
