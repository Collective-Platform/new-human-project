## MOVEMENT 4 — New Generosity

# The outward life of the new humanity

Days 20–25 · Philippians 4:10–23

---

The letter ends with something unexpected: a sustained reflection on money, giving, and generosity. Paul weaves together his thanks for the Philippians' support with a theology of giving that is unlike anything the world offers. These final six days draw Philippians to its close and draw together the whole vision of the New Human Project: a community defined not by what it accumulates, but by what it gives away.
