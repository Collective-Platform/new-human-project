## MOVEMENT 3 — New Peace

# The inner life of the new humanity

Days 14–19 · Philippians 3:15–4:9

---

The new humanity is called not just to act differently, but to think differently. These six days explore the interior life of the congregation Christ is building — how we handle disagreement, where we find our stability, and how the peace of God that passes understanding becomes a living reality rather than a slogan.
