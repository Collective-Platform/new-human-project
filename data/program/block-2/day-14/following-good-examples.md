## Today's Focus

The new humanity is shaped by the people it chooses to imitate.

## Reading Notes

Paul says something that might make us uncomfortable. He tells us to follow his example and watch those who live the same way. He isn't being arrogant. He is just being honest about how we grow. We become like the people we spend time around. The new humanity is shaped by the examples we choose to honor and copy. But Paul also warns us, with tears, about people who only care about earthly things. He calls them enemies of the cross. Our true citizenship is in heaven. We are like a colony of the future living in the present. Our real home and real hope are not here, but our lives right now are shaped entirely by that future.

## Key Idea

_The new humanity is shaped by who it imitates. Choose your examples carefully and be the kind of example worth imitating._

## Reflection

Who are the people in your life whose faith you most want to imitate? What is it about their example that draws you?

## Today's Practice

Tell one person in your church specifically what it is about their walk with Christ that you want to imitate. Make it concrete and personal.
