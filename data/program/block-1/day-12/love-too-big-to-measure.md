## Today's Focus

Paul's great prayer — and the love at the centre of the new humanity.

## Reading Notes

Paul kneels to pray — an unusual posture that signals the weight of what he's about to ask. He prays for three things: inner strength by the Spirit, Christ feeling at home in their hearts, and the capacity to grasp how wide and long and high and deep Christ's love is. Four dimensions — but the point isn't to measure it. The point is that it can't be measured. This love is not a warm feeling; it's the same love that drove Jesus to create the new humanity through the cross. To really know it is to be filled with the fullness of God himself.

## Key Idea

The new humanity is held together not by rules or culture, but by a love too big to get to the bottom of.

## Reflection

Do you find it easy or difficult to believe that God loves you personally and deeply? What gets in the way?

## Today's Practice

Pray Paul's prayer for yourself today. Ask God to help you grasp — not just know about — how much you are loved.
