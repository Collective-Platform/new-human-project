## Today's Focus

Gifts aren't for personal gain — they're for building the whole new humanity up.

## Reading Notes

Christ gave gifts to the church in the form of gifted people: apostles, prophets, evangelists, pastors and teachers. Their job is not to do all the ministry themselves — it's to equip everyone else to do it. The new humanity grows when every member plays their part, not when a few professionals carry the whole load. The goal is maturity — a community so grounded in truth and love that it can't be knocked off course. Mature communities speak truth in love and build each other up. This is what the New Human Project looks like from the inside.

## Key Idea

The new humanity grows when everyone uses their gifts — not when a few people do everything.

## Reflection

What gift do you bring to the body of Christ? Are you using it — or has it been sitting on the shelf?

## Today's Practice

Tell someone in your church this week what gift you see in them. And ask someone what gift they see in you.
