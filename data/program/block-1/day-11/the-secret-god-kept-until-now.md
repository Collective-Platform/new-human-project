## Today's Focus

The mystery revealed: the gospel is for everyone, and the church proves it.

## Reading Notes

Paul calls the inclusion of the Gentiles a "mystery" and he doesn't mean something weird or spooky. He means something that was hidden for a long time and has now finally been uncovered. Here's what was revealed: everyone gets to be fully part of God's family. Not as guests. Not as second-class members who have to sit at the kids' table. As full heirs, with the same inheritance.

But it goes even further than that. The church's unity across every kind of difference is actually a message, sent to the entire universe, that God's plan to put everything back together is real and working. The New Humanity isn't just a community that exists for its own benefit. It's a living announcement that something genuinely new has started in the world.

## Key Idea

The diversity in your church isn't a management problem or an awkward tension to smooth over. It's the whole point a real-time demonstration to the world that the gospel actually does what it claims to do.

## Reflection

What does the mix of people in your church say about the gospel right now? And what might it need to say more clearly?

## Today's Practice

Pray for your church today not for a programme or an upcoming event, but for real, genuine unity across every difference that would normally keep people apart.
