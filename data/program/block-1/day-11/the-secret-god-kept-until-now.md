## Today's Focus

The mystery revealed: the gospel is for everyone, and the church proves it.

## Reading Notes

Paul calls the inclusion of the Gentiles a 'mystery' — not something strange or secret, but something hidden in previous generations and now finally disclosed. The revelation is this: everyone is fully included in God's family, not as guests or second-tier members, but as fellow heirs. And the purpose goes even further — the church's unity is meant to be a message to the whole universe that God's reconciling plan is real. The new humanity is not just a community for its own sake. It is a living announcement that the New Human Project is underway.

## Key Idea

Your church's diversity is not a problem to manage. It's the point — a living demonstration to the world that the gospel works.

## Reflection

What does the mix of people in your church say about the gospel? What might it need to say more clearly?

## Today's Practice

Pray for your church today — not for a program or event, but for genuine unity across every difference.
