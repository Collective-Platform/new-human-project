## Today's Focus

Through Jesus, every person — regardless of background — comes to God the same way.

## Reading Notes

Verse 18 is quietly stunning: through Christ, by the Spirit, both groups now approach the Father. Same door. Same access. Nobody gets a VIP entrance. Nobody is turned away because of where they come from. The New Human Project has no second-class citizens — everyone enters through the same Jesus. And this access is not just vertical (us and God) — it's horizontal (us and each other). You can't genuinely claim to be close to God while keeping other people at arm's length. The two go together.

## Key Idea

There are no second-class citizens in God's new humanity. Everyone gets the same access, through the same Jesus.

## Reflection

Is there anyone you've treated — consciously or unconsciously — as less welcome in God's presence than you are?

## Today's Practice

Meditate on the word 'access' today. You have been given an open invitation into the Father's presence. Use it.
