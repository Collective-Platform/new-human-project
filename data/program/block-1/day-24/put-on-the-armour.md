## Today's Focus

The new humanity is in a real battle — and God has already provided everything needed to stand firm.

## Reading Notes

Paul ends the letter where he began — with a cosmic perspective. The New Human Project faces real opposition. Behind broken systems, persistent temptation, and relentless discouragement, there are spiritual forces at work. So God gives us armour — but notice, it's mostly defensive. The call is to 'stand firm,' not to charge ahead alone. Truth, righteousness, the gospel of peace, faith, salvation, the Word of God — each piece is something God provides, not something we manufacture. And woven through all of it is prayer: pray at all times, for all the saints. The new humanity stands together or it doesn't stand at all.

## Key Idea

The new humanity is in a real battle — but not unarmed, and not alone.

## Reflection

Which piece of the armour do you most consistently forget to put on? What would it look like to do so deliberately each morning?

## Today's Practice

Start tomorrow with a simple prayer that puts on each piece of armour. Use Ephesians 6:14–18 as your guide.
